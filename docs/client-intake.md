# Client Intake Form

Use this to collect the minimum useful information for a client-ready small business website.

## Business Basics

- Business name
- Industry
- Main city or town
- Cities, towns, neighborhoods, or counties served
- Public phone number
- Public email address
- Public address, if customers should see it
- Website domain, if already owned

## Positioning

- What headline or slogan should customers see first?
- In 2-3 sentences, what does the business do?
- What makes the business trustworthy?
- Should the tone feel serious, friendly, premium, rugged, or compassionate?

## Services

- What services do you offer?
- Which 2-3 services are most important?
- Are there emergency or urgent services?
- Do you offer recurring maintenance, plans, packages, or consultations?

## Calls To Action

- Should customers call, request a quote, book a consultation, or choose either?
- What phone number should customers call?
- Where should form submissions go?
- Do you use a scheduling link, Formspree endpoint, Netlify Forms, or email only?

## Proof

- Do you have project photos?
- Do you have before/after images?
- Do you have testimonials or Google reviews?
- Do you have licenses, certifications, years in business, warranties, or community memberships?

## Pages

- Home
- Services
- About / Trust
- Gallery / Reviews
- Contact

Optional upgrades:

- Process page
- Pricing or package page
- FAQ page
- Resource hub
- Individual service-area pages

## Assets

- Logo, if available
- Brand colors, if any
- Photos
- Social links
- Existing website link, if replacing an old site
